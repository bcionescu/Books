# Synopsis

    <p>
      <i>How does technology shape culture, and by extension, human behavior? In the near future, humans receive a neuralbond implant at birth, making it possible to communicate via thought.</i>
    </p>
    <p>
      While at first this is a luxury, soon it becomes a necessity. As the majority of the population adopts the technology, speech becomes taboo, and it is eventually outlawed.
    </p>
    <p>
      Claudius races against time to solve a murder at the hands of religious fanatics, or he will lose that which matters most to him.
    </p>
