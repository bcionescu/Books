## Prologue

Ambrosia’s sweat-soaked dress clings to her body as the soldiers chase us.

Holding her hand as we run, I turn my head, aiming my Atomizer at the nearest soldier, and press the trigger. The man freezes in midair for a moment before turning into a fine red mist.

We continue our desperate gallop, our chests heaving, legs aching. I can see it, the vehicle is right where it should be, hidden in the trees at the end of the field.

Without looking, I aim the Atomizer behind me, shooting aimlessly. Judging by the screaming, I managed to hit at least one more of them.

As we reach the edge of the forest, I help Ambrosia get inside the vehicle, jumping into my own seat immediately after. Looking around in a frenzy, I press the ignition button. The vehicle roars, producing a sound that does not bode well, as the engine dies with a tremor.
